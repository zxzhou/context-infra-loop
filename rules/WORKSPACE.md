# Workspace Routing

## Main Areas

- `rules/`: durable instructions, axioms, skills, and operating preferences.
- `contexts/`: memory, research sessions, daily records, and temporary knowledge artifacts.
- `tools/`: reusable local utilities.
- `periodic_jobs/`: scheduled automation and heartbeat jobs.
- `adhoc_jobs/`: standalone experiments or one-off tools.

## Routing Rule

Put stable reusable guidance in `rules/`, task evidence in `contexts/`, executable utilities in `tools/`, scheduled automation in `periodic_jobs/`, and experiments in `adhoc_jobs/`.
